Privacy Policy

MindPulse is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name and email address when you register.
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, actions taken, device and browser information.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve user experience and analyze site usage. See our Cookie Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To facilitate bookings with counselors.
  • To communicate with you about your account and bookings.
  • To analyze usage and improve platform features.
  • To comply with legal requirements.

3. Sharing Your Information

Your privacy is paramount. We do not share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to provide our services (e.g., connecting you with a university counselor you booked) or as required by law.

4. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

  • You may update your account information at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your account by contacting support.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page and are effective immediately.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@mindpulse.com.
